Taking away the throw away.

The togo project is a window display campaign to help bring awareness around the impacts of fast consumption and invigorate community action towards more conscious choices. By taking perfectly good togo wares out of the waste-stream and constructing them into painstakingly beautiful art installations, we can slowly create a better world inside and out.
