    how is this connected to the global community?

    Our use of togo products effects our shared resources on a global level. Think of a wooden chopstick you would use one time... How many tons of chopsticks are transported during one year? How much fossil fuel is used? Were does the wood come from? Is that land over harvested? Is it a by-product of another manufacturing process?

    Or if we look at polystyrene foam (trademarked as Styrofoam) we see it can be unhealthy for the workers exposed to its making, it pollutes the air in both its creation and recycling.

    These items are used one time so even though we can’t fathom each impact of each piece of to go ware, perhaps we can agree that disposable products aren’t always necessary in the first place.

    how is this connected to chicago?

    We see refuse littered around the city everyday. It isn't gorgeous. We know that we can consume Chicago's amazing offerings in a more conscious manner. It will make us feel good AND make our city look better.

    how is this connected to businesses?

    Businesses can decrease their consumption and create connections within their community by becoming engaged in this conversation! Soon we will begin helping small businesses see how they can creatively lessen their environmental impacts...stay tuned.

    how is this connected to individuals?

    Everyone creates waste. Some of it is unnecessary. The more individuals we have working together, the more your actions will inspire others and the more will be accomplished!
